guest - person, who is not logged in
user - logged in person
administrator - user with extra rights
rating - non-negative intenger, used for ranking of users
round/contest - competition conducted at a fixed time by specific rules
rated round - round, by results of which the recount of rating occurs
unrated round - round, results of which do not affect on rating
friend - user, which another user follows
rank - one of possible titles, that user has, depending on his rating
division - separation of users depending on their rating
user's profile - page with the major information about user
blog - set of user's posts
team - a few users, that are able to participate in rounds together
